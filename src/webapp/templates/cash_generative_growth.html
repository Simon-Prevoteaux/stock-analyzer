Cash-Generative Growth

High-growth stocks that generate positive free cash flow - a rare and valuable combination

What is Cash-Generative Growth?

Cash-generative growth is the rare combination of high revenue growth alongside positive and growing free cash flow. Most high-growth companies burn cash to fuel expansion, making FCF-positive growth stocks particularly valuable.

Why It's Valuable:

  • Lower Risk: FCF-positive companies don't rely on external financing to fund operations
  • Sustainable Growth: Growth funded by internal cash generation is more sustainable
  • Optionality: Cash gives management flexibility for acquisitions, buybacks, or dividends
  • Rare Combination: Most growth stocks burn cash; FCF-positive growth is uncommon

Key Metrics:

FCF Margin ≥ 10%

Strong cash generation relative to revenue

Cash Conversion > 0.8

Earnings efficiently converted to cash (FCF / Net Income > 80%)

Revenue Growth ≥ 20%

High-growth while maintaining positive FCF
