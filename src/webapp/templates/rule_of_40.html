What is the Rule of 40?

The Rule of 40 states that a company's combined revenue growth rate and profit margin should equal or exceed 40%. It's a key performance indicator for SaaS and cloud companies that balances growth with profitability.

Formula: Revenue Growth Rate (%) + Free Cash Flow Margin (%) ≥ 40

≥ 40: Excellent

Efficient growth, balancing expansion with profitability

20-40: Good

Acceptable performance, room for improvement

< 20: Low

Inefficient growth or profitability concerns

Default: 40 (the threshold)
